MEMORANDUM
TO: All Department Heads
FROM: Office of the CEO
DATE: May 2024
SUBJECT: Strategic AI Acceleration

As we look toward 2025, speed of implementation is our primary competitive advantage. While we value the flexibility of open-source projects, we are now prioritizing commercial, enterprise-grade platforms that offer 24/7 support, guaranteed uptime, and immediate integration capabilities. For any mission-critical AI applications, commercial solutions are now acceptable and often preferred if they reduce time-to-market.
